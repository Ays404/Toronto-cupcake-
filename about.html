About Toronto Cupcake

Our story, our passion, our commitment to quality

Our Sweet Beginning

Toronto Cupcake was born out of a passion for baking and a desire to bring joy to people's lives through delicious treats. What started as a small home-based business in 2010 has grown into one of Toronto's most beloved cupcake destinations.

Our founder, Michelle Harrison, began baking cupcakes for friends and family gatherings, and the overwhelming positive response encouraged her to turn her hobby into a full-fledged business. With dedication to quality and a flair for creative designs, Toronto Cupcake quickly gained popularity.

Today, we continue to uphold the values that made us successful: using only the finest ingredients, baking fresh daily, and providing exceptional customer service. Every cupcake is made with love and attention to detail, ensuring that each bite is as delightful as the first.
