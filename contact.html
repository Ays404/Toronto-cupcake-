Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I place my order?

For standard orders, we recommend placing your order at least 48 hours in advance. For custom designs or large orders, please allow 3-5 business days. Rush orders may be accommodated based on availability.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery throughout Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area. Delivery fees vary based on location and order size. Please contact us for specific delivery rates to your area.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer vegan, gluten-free, and nut-free options. Please specify any dietary requirements when placing your order, and we'll be happy to accommodate your needs.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled up to 48 hours before the scheduled delivery or pickup time. Please contact us as soon as possible to make any changes to your order.

Do you offer custom designs for corporate events?

Absolutely! We specialize in corporate branding and can create cupcakes featuring your company logo, colors, or custom designs. Please contact our corporate events team for more information.
